Bright Ideas: Energy Saving Tips

Small changes can make a big difference in your energy consumption and bills.

Switch to LEDs

Replace incandescent bulbs with energy-efficient LEDs. They last longer and use significantly less energy.

Unplug Devices

Many electronics consume power even when off (phantom load). Unplug them or use smart power strips.

Smart Thermostats

Install a programmable or smart thermostat to optimize your heating and cooling schedules efficiently.

Consider Solar

Explore solar panel installation to generate your own clean energy and reduce reliance on the grid.
